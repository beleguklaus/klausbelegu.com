About Me

A bit about my background and homelab journey.

Hi, I'm Klaus!

I'm an IT professional working at an MSP, where I love helping businesses leverage technologies to solve real-world challenges. My focus lies in improving ineffecient processes via cloud or automation. I also have a heavy passion in properly configuring and deploying cloud software/systems.

My main skills include the full Microsoft Stack, PowerShell/Scripting, Cloud software/systems, Azure, Windows/Linux servers, RMM solutions and more.

This portfolio showcases some of the hybrid projects I've been working on as I explore different services and capabilities. I'm passionate about creating efficient, scalable, and secure solutions.

My home lab consists of a Dell PowerEdge running Proxmox with numerous LXC containers and Linux VMs (some windows VM for testing).

My on-prem solution is behind my Procteli 4-port Vault running OPNSense, configured with proper network segmentation and inbound/outbound rules.

I have implemented an Azure VPN gateway to connect up to my Azure home lab but generally is not in play due to high Azure costs.

I also have my on-premise infrastructure, including workstation level backed up to my QNAP NAS. For my cloud solutions, I utilize Azure backup.

When I'm not working with my homelab, I enjoy staying up-to-date with the latest tech trends and findin g open-source projects to explore.

I am currently working on my pathway to the Azure Solutions Architect Expert certification as I believe this is where most MSPs have opportunity growth in.
